Welcome to ChessRPG!
ChessRPG is a board game with chess-based rules, there you can customize your figures with: trace, distance, attack and defense they have.
(Currently available for all figures except pawns.). In this version players can create, rename and customize boards for offline mode sessions

[Team]:
Click on team to customize it
Table in right side for customization of figures in curent team
Possible values:
Attack: [0,9]
Defense: [0,9]
0 in attack means that figure can only move
0 in defense - figure can be killed by any enemy
Trace: [forward],[line],[diagonal],[crosshair],[horse],[queen]
Forward - move forward in available distances
Line - move forward and backward in available distances
Diagonal - move like a bishop in available distances
Crosshair - move horizontal and vertical in available distances
Horse - move like a horse(knight), only L-shape moves
Queen - move in any direction in available distances

To rename and delete teams, double-click on team.To create new, press [Create new] and rename [BLANK TEAM]

[GAME]:
To check available moves, click on the figure.
To make a move choose two figures, from and to you want to move.
If yours attack is lower than enemy's defense, enemy will be damaged, figures will not move, your turn will go to enemy.
In order to win you need to clearly cut down the enemy's king.
To change field size, press on [SIZE-BUTTONS] on the right.
Size changes takes up to 15 seconds to reload the field, please be patient.
To quit game session double-click on [QUIT].
